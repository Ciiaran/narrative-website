Buyers Guide

Introduction Best Air fryers

Whether you're a first-time air-fryer shopper, a recent buyer, or a long-time owner, this will show you the best air fryer to buy. Using the reviews based off the bbcgoodfood website thye have gone through tests for making chips, roasting chicken, grilling halloumi and aubergine, and, when possible, baking cake to assess all functions. And have focused on achieving evenly browned chips with a crisp exterior and excellent flavor before seasoning in each model tested.

Air Fryer One Lakeland dual-basket air-fryer

Advantages:

  • Sleek appearance
  • Outstanding cooking performance
  • Comes with a 3-year warranty
  • Convenient easy-view windows for monitoring ingredients while cooking

Disadvantages:

  • Dimly lit controls
  • Some icons may be challenging to identify

Overall Rating: 4.5/5 stars

Air Fryer Two Cosori TurboBlaze air-fryer

Advantages:

  • Elegant and modern design
  • User-friendly touchscreen display
  • Efficient and quiet cooking performance
  • Clear and well-documented manual

Disadvantages:

  • Prone to showing fingerprints
  • Occupies significant space

Overall Rating: 5/5 stars

Air Fryer Three Ninja Foodi MAX Dual Zone

Advantages:

  • Generous cooking capacity
  • Simple and efficient cleaning process
  • Dual cooking zones for versatile meal preparation
  • Programmable feature ensures synchronized cooking of various foods

Disadvantages:

  • Occupies substantial counter space due to its large footprint
  • Lacks pre-set cooking options tailored for specific ingredients

Overall Rating: 5/5 stars
